About UAC Services

Your trusted supplier of car wash equipment for petrol stations

Who We Are

UAC Services supplies car wash equipment and cleaning supplies to petrol stations across Cape Town and throughout South Africa. Our products are used for cleaning cars and windscreens at petrol station forecourts. We specialize in providing high-quality, durable supplies that withstand the demands of busy forecourt environments.

What We Supply

We provide everything petrol stations need for car and windscreen cleaning:

  • Premium squeegees in multiple colors for windscreen cleaning
  • Industrial-grade garage rolls (paper towels) for car cleaning
  • Professional-quality car wash soaps and cleaning chemicals
  • Replacement rubbers and sponges for squeegees
  • Car cleaning accessories and supplies

Who We Serve

We serve petrol stations across Cape Town that need reliable car wash supplies for their forecourts. Our customers use our squeegees, soaps, and paper towels to help drivers keep their vehicles and windscreens clean. We understand the unique needs of petrol stations and are committed to providing quality car cleaning products.

Our Commitment

We pride ourselves on reliability, quality, and competitive pricing. With generous bulk discounts, fast delivery across Cape Town, and responsive customer service, we make it easy for petrol stations to keep their car wash supplies fully stocked.
